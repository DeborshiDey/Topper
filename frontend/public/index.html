About Us

Based in Sribhumi, Assam, Topper is an Indian stationery enterprise dedicated to building great, sustainable notebooks at an affordable price. We design thoughtful products that make everyday writing, sketching, and planning a joy.

Our promise is simple: responsibly sourced materials, reliable build quality, and fair pricing—so more learners and creators can bring ideas to life.

Sustainable Materials

Responsibly sourced paper and recycled packaging to reduce waste.

Durable Build

Sturdy binding, smooth finish, and bleed‑resistant pages.

Fair Pricing

Premium quality made accessible for students and professionals.
